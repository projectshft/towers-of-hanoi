Towers of Hanoi

The goal is to move a set of discs of different sizes, ranging in size from the smallest (1) the largest(5), from one peg to another. You can only move the topmost disc off the peg and you can't put a larger disc on top of a smaller one. You win when you put all the discs back in their original order on a DIFFERENT peg. The game has three pegs but if you want an easier challenge, add a row by clicking the 'Add Row' button.
