Towers of Hanoi

Open your console to play the game

to open your console:

  • Press "Command" + "Shift" + J" on Macs
  • Press "CTRL" + "Shift" + "J" on Windows
  • Right Click on your mouse and select "inspect"

To begin, type into the console: my.move('num', 'num') where num are numbers between 1 and 5. the first 'num' represents your starting tower, and the second represents the tower you are sending your "disc" to. The game will automatically alert you once you have won. Have fun!
